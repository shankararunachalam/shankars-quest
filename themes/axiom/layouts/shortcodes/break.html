{{- /* break.html — the axiom theme's trail-adaptive section break. Resolves a two-level lookup against data/breaks.yaml: trail - the post's `trails` front-matter field; falls back to "journal" when unset or unknown. variant - the optional first argument; falls back to the trail's default when omitted, and when an unknown variant is passed.
